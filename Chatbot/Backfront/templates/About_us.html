About Us

Our Mission

This website is a social initiative aimed at helping animals in need. Many animals die due to a lack of resources and medical treatment. Our platform bridges the gap between individuals who want to help but lack the means and NGOs that are capable of helping but struggle to locate animals in need.

Report Injured Animal

Our app connects local people and NGOs, ensuring timely assistance for injured and stray animals. Users can report cases, submit locations, and get immediate responses from nearby NGOs willing to assist.

Chatbot Assistance

We provide an interactive chatbot where users can ask for guidance on treating animals. This enables them to offer immediate aid before professional help arrives.

Real-Time Chat & Forum

Users can engage in discussions about animal welfare, share knowledge, and even raise funds. Our platform fosters a community of like-minded individuals passionate about animal rescue.
